Hello! I'm here to help you organize your tasks!

1

Adding a Category

Type a category name in the input field at the top and click Add Category or press Enter. For example: "School", "Work", or "Personal".

2

Adding Tasks

Type your task in the input field within any category and click "Add Task" or press Enter. Tasks are added instantly!

3

Editing Items

Use the icon to edit either a category title or an individual task. A modal will appear for editing.

4

Deleting Items

Use the icon to delete categories or tasks. You'll be asked to confirm before anything is deleted.

5

Data Storage

All your categories and tasks are automatically saved in your browser, so they'll be there when you come back!
